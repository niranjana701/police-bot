THEFT COMPLAINT:
Category: Theft / Stolen Property
Priority: MEDIUM
Urgency: Medium
Required Documents: Aadhaar card, FIR request letter, proof of ownership, IMEI number (for phones)
Route to: Nearest police station, crime branch
Action: File within 24 hours

CYBERCRIME COMPLAINT:
Category: Cybercrime / Online Fraud
Priority: HIGH
Urgency: High
Required Documents: Screenshot of fraud, bank transaction details, Aadhaar card, email/phone records
Route to: Cyber Crime Cell, cybercrime.gov.in
Action: File immediately, freeze bank account if money lost

HARASSMENT COMPLAINT:
Category: Harassment / Threatening
Priority: HIGH
Urgency: High
Required Documents: Aadhaar card, witness details, screenshots or call records, written complaint
Route to: Local police station, women helpline 1091 if applicable
Action: File immediately

ACCIDENT COMPLAINT:
Category: Road Accident / Vehicle Damage
Priority: HIGH
Urgency: High
Required Documents: Vehicle RC, insurance papers, driving license, photos of accident scene
Route to: Traffic police, nearest police station
Action: File within 6 hours

MISSING PERSON COMPLAINT:
Category: Missing Person
Priority: VERY HIGH
Urgency: Very High
Required Documents: Recent photograph, Aadhaar card, last known location details, contact info
Route to: Nearest police station immediately
Action: File immediately, call 100

PROPERTY DISPUTE COMPLAINT:
Category: Property Dispute / Land Grabbing
Priority: MEDIUM
Urgency: Medium
Required Documents: Property documents, Aadhaar card, survey records, witness names
Route to: Civil court or local police station
Action: File within 48 hours

DOMESTIC VIOLENCE COMPLAINT:
Category: Domestic Violence / Family Abuse
Priority: HIGH
Urgency: High
Required Documents: Aadhaar card, medical reports if any, witness details, photos of injury
Route to: Local police station, women helpline 1091, one stop centre
Action: File immediately, call 112 for emergency

NOISE POLLUTION COMPLAINT:
Category: Noise Pollution / Public Nuisance
Priority: LOW
Urgency: Low
Required Documents: Written complaint, address proof, date and time of incident
Route to: Local police station, municipality office
Action: File within 7 days
